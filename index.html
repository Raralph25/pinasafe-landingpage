About PinaSafe

PinaSafe is a mobile app that helps citizens report emergencies and disasters in real-time. Our mission is to keep communities safe and connected during critical times through instant alerts and community-driven reporting.

Features

Real-time Incident Reporting

Verified & Categorized Reports

Barangay-based Response System

AI-Powered Disaster Image Detection

Interactive Live Maps

Instant Community Alerts

How It Works

Step 1: Report an incident with location and media.
Step 2: AI and personnel verify the report.
Step 3: Receive help and get updates instantly.
